Signs Your Battery Needs Replacing

⚡
Slow engine crank

If your engine is turning over slowly when you start, the battery is losing capacity.

💡
Battery warning light

The battery warning light on your dashboard means the charging system needs attention.

❄️
Cold weather starting problems

Batteries fail more often in cold weather — if your car struggles in winter, don't wait for a no-start.

🔦
Dimming electrics

Lights, radio or heaters losing power points to battery or charging system issues.
