Our Story

Born From Frustration With Traditional Garages

Tech & Tyres was founded on a simple insight: getting your tyres changed or your car serviced shouldn't require half a day's disruption. Traditional garages mean booking in advance, arranging alternative transport, and sitting in a waiting room.

We set out to change that — bringing professional, qualified service directly to Melksham homes and businesses. Today, thousands of drivers across Wiltshire, North Somerset and Bristol trust us to keep their vehicles in top condition without the hassle.

Our Melksham depot provides a fixed base for customers who prefer to come to us, while our mobile units cover a wide area for those who'd rather not.
